# 📚 AAT — AI Academic Tutor

AAT (AI Academic Tutor) is a next-generation open-source educational platform designed to help students learn, organize, and improve their academic performance using Artificial Intelligence.

Inspired by modern educational experiences, AAT combines AI tutoring, personalized learning, smart reminders, quizzes, flashcards, study planning, voice interactions, camera scanning, image generation, and collaborative learning into a single platform.

Our goal is simple:

> Help students study smarter, not harder.

---

# ✨ Vision

Most educational applications focus on either content or organization.

AAT combines both.

The platform acts as:

- AI Tutor
- Study Planner
- Homework Assistant
- Exam Preparation Coach
- Flashcard Generator
- Quiz Generator
- Academic Companion

All inside a modern, student-focused experience.

---

# 🤖 AI Academic Assistant

Students can chat naturally with AAT.

Example:

User:
> I have a biology exam tomorrow at 4:40 PM, remind me.

AAT:
> Sure! I've created a reminder.
>
> Would you like to:
> - Practice with a quiz?
> - Review flashcards?
> - Generate a summary?
> - Create a study plan?

The assistant is designed to transform simple requests into productive study sessions.
